India inspires our imaginations to run wild with vivid thoughts of pristine wilderness and

exceptional wildlife. Immersing ourselves in a land amongst the calm waters of the

Okavango Delta or to the unspoilt wildlife area of Liuwa Plains

As we strive to travel on a safari at least once in our lifetime.….we connect culture and

wildlife together showcasing a journey with a difference. Our knowledge, passion,

expertise and love of personal experiences drives us to know all about the India in you.

Our tailored African safaris designed by Reis add an element of sustainable tourism,

without harming our planet. Its official the "Green Safari" is here with all our heart and

soul…..we look forward to seeing you soon. Justine
